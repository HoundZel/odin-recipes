Pizza



Description

Pizza is a savory dish of Italian origin consisting of a usually round, flattened base of leavened wheat-based dough topped with tomatoes, cheese, and often various other ingredients (such as anchovies, mushrooms, onions, olives, pineapple, meat, etc.), which is then baked at a high temperature, traditionally in a wood-fired oven.


Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups warm water (110 degrees F/45 degrees C)
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 2 1/4 teaspoons active dry yeast
  • 3 1/2 cups bread flour
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 1/4 cup cornmeal
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 1 1/2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 cup sliced pepperoni
  • 1/2 cup sliced black olives
  • 1/2 cup sliced green bell pepper
  • 1/2 cup sliced fresh mushrooms
  • 1/4 cup sliced onion
  • 1/4 cup sliced ham
  • 1/4 cup sliced pineapple

Instructions

  1. In a small bowl, dissolve sugar in warm water. Sprinkle yeast over the top, and let stand for about 10 minutes, until foamy.
  2. In a large bowl, combine flour, olive oil, salt, and the yeast mixture. Mix well and knead to form a smooth dough. Let it rest for about 1 hour, until doubled in size.
  3. Preheat oven to 475 degrees F (245 degrees C). Divide dough in half, and spread into two round pans. Sprinkle some cornmeal on the pans before placing the dough.
  4. Spread pizza sauce over each round, then layer with mozzarella cheese, Parmesan cheese, and your favorite toppings.
  5. Bake in preheated oven for 20 to 25 minutes, or until crust is golden brown.
  6. Serve
